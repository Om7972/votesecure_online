Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to verify your identity and ensure the integrity of the voting process. This may include:

  • Personal Identification Information (Name, Government ID number)
  • Biometric Data (Facial scan data, strictly for verification purposes)
  • Contact Information (Email address, Phone number)
  • Device and Usage Data (IP address, Browser type)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for:

  • Verifying your eligibility to vote.
  • Processing your encrypted vote.
  • Generating a verifiable audit trail on the blockchain.
  • Detecting and preventing fraud.

3. Data Security & Encryption

We implement military-grade AES-256 encryption. Your vote is anonymized before being recorded on the blockchain. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
