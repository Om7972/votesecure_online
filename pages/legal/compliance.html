Regulatory Compliance

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

VoteSecure Online is committed to maintaining the highest standards of regulatory compliance for election integrity.

GDPR Compliant

Full compliance with EU General Data Protection Regulation for data handling and the "Right to be Forgotten".

SOC 2 Type II

Independently audited controls for security, availability, and confidentiality.

ISO 27001

Certified Information Security Management System (ISMS).

WCAG 2.1 AA

Fully accessible interface for users with disabilities.

Audit Trails

Every transaction and vote is cryptographically signed and logged. We maintain these immutable logs for a minimum of 7 years as required by federal election guidelines.
